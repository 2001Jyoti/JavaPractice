Write an algorithm to find the sum of first n even numbers(2,4,6,8,.....).
sol:
step1:start
step2:read the values
step3: initialize sum<--0,i<--2,count<--1
step4:repeatedly execute the steps (i)-(iii) until (count<=n)
         i)sum<--sum+i
         ii)i<--i+2
         iii)count<--count+1
step5: print sum
step6:stop
